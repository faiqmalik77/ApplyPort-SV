Research Proposal — Template (replace bracketed hints)

1. Title & summary
   - Working title and 2–3 sentences on the problem and intended contribution.

2. Background & literature
   - Context, gaps in existing work, and how your idea fits.

3. Objectives & questions
   - Clear aims or research questions you will address.

4. Methodology
   - Data, methods, experiments, or theoretical approach; feasibility.

5. Expected outcomes
   - What success looks like and how it advances the field or practice.

6. Timeline & resources (if required)
   - Major phases and any equipment, data, or supervision needs.

Align terminology with the host department and cite key sources where appropriate.
